Home Shahada Salat Sawm Zakat Hajj

This is the last pillar of Islam, it is the pilgrimage of a believer to the Holy land, where the prayer house of the father of All nations(Kaaba) is found
and also to the holy land of Medina. Hajj is an advanced way of connecting with Allah and repenting to Him. It is required only once in a lifetime. Islam teaches that any believer who has the ability to perform Hajj. According to Islam, When one performs Hajj sincerely and accurately all his or her sins are wiped away and it also increases his Imaan(Faith).A person who has performed
Hajj is called Alhajj for a man and Alhajia for a woman. Hajj is performed in the month of Dhul-Hijra and is mostly companied with celebration of Eid-ul-Adha.
