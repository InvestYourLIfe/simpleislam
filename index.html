Introduction

Islam is a monotheistic religion which focuses on mankind
obtaining peace by submitting themselves to the Almighty God(Allah).
The basic fundamentals of Islam are five,
they are popularly known as the pillars of Islam. These are;

  1. Khalimat Shahada(Declaration of Faith fully)
  2. Salat(Prayer)
  3. Sawn(fasting in the month of Ramadan)
  4. Zakat(Giving of Alms to the needy)
  5. Hajj(pilgrimage to the Mecca)

Islam also has basic rules and principals based on some sources.
These sources are;

  1. The Holy Quran(The Holy word of Allah)
  2. The Hadiths(The sayings of the Holy Prophet Mohammed)
  3. The Sunnas(The actions of the Holy Prophet Mohammed)

Other books Islam believes in are;

  1. Torah(the book revealed to Prophet Musa/Moses)
  2. Zabur/Psalms(the book revealed to Prophet Dawud/David)
  3. Injil/Gospel(the book revealed to Prophet Isaah/Jesus)

The main aim of islam is for mankind to;

  • Worship and Submit themselves fully to Allah only
  • Acquire Internal Peace
  • Gain Jannah(Paradise)
  • Develop love for one another
