The Salat is a form of prayer in Islam, where by a person takes specific times of the day to draw his or her mind and soul closer to Allah,
thereby preventing them from engaging in evil acts, it believed that if a person constantly performs Salat and still engages in evil acts
then it means there is something wrong with his or her salat. Salat is basically performed to reprogramme one's mind towards Allah by reciting Suras of
the Holy Quran, bowing and prostrating to Allah and praising and seeking His forgiveness and mercy. Salat should be performed with full attention.
Abolution should be performed before Salat, and Salat should be observed only in the Arabic language. The only time a person is exempted from Salat is when
she is undergoing her monthly ovulation. Salat is performed facing the prayer house(kaaba) of The Father of All Nations(Abraham) in Mecca while exhibiting a form
of Unity among people as they face one direction in a circular manner and use one language in their Salat. The Salat is grouped into the following types;

  • Fard Salat(compulsary prayers)
  • Nawfil Salat(optional prayers)
  • Special Salat

Islam also has other types of prayers apart from Salat which are;

  • Zikir of Allah(constant remembrance of Allah all the time)
  • Making Duwah(pleading with Allah)

Fard Salat(compulsary prayers)

These are the five daily prayers in Islam which should be observed each and every day. These prayers are so important that even if one is disabled
but he or she must offer this prayer even if they have to sit while perfoming it. The five daily prayers are shown below

Prayer Rakats Time of the Day
Fajr 2 Before sunrise
Zuhr 4 Sun at highest point
Asr 4 Sun begins to come down
Maghrib 3 Before Sunset
Isha 4 Sun sets completely

Nawfil Salat(optional prayers)

These are the optional but highly recommended Salats in Islam, which people perform to gain increase their Imaan(faith) and gain blessings from Allah.
Some of the Nawfil Prayers are;

  • The night prayer
  • The Sunna prayers
  • The morning prayer
  • Among Others

Special Salat

Islam also have special type prayers performed in special events and occassions.Some of these special salats are;

  • Eid prayers
  • Naming prayers
  • Marriage prayers
  • Taraweeh prayers
  • Hajj prayers
  • Janaza prayers
  • Among Others
