Khalimat Shahada(Declaration of Imaan)

According to Islam, in order to truely have faith one must belief in all the following concepts. These major concepts of faith should be believed entirely.
They are known as the 6 Articles of Faith, and they are;

  • Full Belief in Allah(Almighty God)
  • Full Belief in the Angels of Allah
  • Full Belief in the Holy books of Allah
  • Full Belief in All the Prophets of Allah
  • Full Belief in the Day of Judgement and in Life after Death
  • Full Belief in the Destiny ordained by Allah

Full Belief in Allah(Almighty God)

In Islam, the first and most important article of faith is to believe in and love Allah(The One True Creator) with all one's whole heart and accept all His Attributes.
Islam believes that faith can be decreased by performing bad deeds and can also be increased by performing good deeds with the intention of doing it solely for Allah.

Full Belief in the Angels of Allah

The belief in all the angels of Allah is also require in Islam in order to acquire complete Imaan(Faith). A person should belief totally in the angels of Allah,
especially the arch angels. The arch angels are;

  • The Messenger Angel
  • The Angel of the Trumpet
  • The Angel of the Weather
  • The Angel of Death

Full Belief in the Holy books of Allah

Believing and following the Holy Books of Allah is a key piece in the puzzle of faith in Islam. Mankind is expected to believe and practise the Word of Allah entirely.
The paramount scripture in Islam is The Holy Quran, others too include;

  1. Torah(the book revealed to Prophet Musa/Moses)
  2. Zabur/Psalms(the book revealed to Prophet Dawud/David)
  3. Injil/Gospel(the book revealed to Prophet Isaah/Jesus)

Full Belief in All the Prophets of Allah

Islam teaches that mankind should believe in all the Prophets of Allah who came to spread Tawheed(the Oneness of Allah[The One True God]).
Thus, from Adam to the Last Prophet. Some of the Prophets mentioned in The Holy Quran include;

  1. Prophet Adam(SAW)
  2. Prophet Noah/Nuhu(SAW)
  3. Prophet Abraham/Ibrahim(SAW)
  4. Prophet Moses/Musa(SAW)
  5. Prophet Jesus/Isaah(SAW)
  6. Prophet Mohammed(SAW)
  7. Among others

Full Belief in the Day of Judgement and in Life after Death

Mankind should believe in the Day of Judgement, Life after Death and in Jannah(Paradise) and Jahannam(the Great Fire) and prepare towards that
by pleasing Allah to gain His favour on the Prescribed Day

Full Belief in the Destiny ordained by Allah

People should also believe that destiny and all that which is to happen in the future including what has already past lies in the Hands of Allah only
and All should therefore put their trust in Him.
