Zakat

Home Shahada Salat Sawm Zakat Hajj

This is a task placed on well-to-do people to give 2.5% of their wealth to te poor and needy each lunar year. Zakat is not a choice but a responsbile to
the rich and a right to the poor. Zakat is a way of showing equality among the rich and the poor and allowing the under privilege to also live a comfortable life.
Apart from Zakat there is another way of giving alms voluntarily and it is called Sadaqa(charity).When one givings Zakat the person:

  • Cleanses his wealth
  • Gets some of his sin forgiven
  • Gains blessings from Allah
  • And many more
